X Mutual Radar Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

X Mutual Radar values your privacy. This browser extension is designed to process social relationship data locally within your browser. It does NOT upload your account information, tweet content, or private relationship data to developer servers or any third parties.

1. Data the Extension Reads

This extension operates exclusively on https://x.com/* pages and reads only publicly visible information necessary to identify author relationships, specifically:

  • Author Handle: The public username of the account being viewed.
  • Public Relationship Labels: Status indicators (e.g., "Follows you") provided by the X platform.
  • Public Engagement Counts: Follower and following counts available on account profiles.
  • Local Preferences: Your extension-specific settings such as display toggles and UI positions.

2. How Data is Processed

All data is processed strictly in your local browser environment for the following purposes:

  • To identify if an account is a mutual follower with your current account.
  • To display markers for one-way follow relationships.
  • To calculate and display following-to-follower ratios.
  • To render UI elements (tags and highlights) on the page according to your preferences.

3. Data Transfer and Storage

Is data uploaded to a server?

No. This extension does not communicate with any external developer server. Your account credentials, follow lists, browsed content, and relationship data never leave your local device.

Local Storage

The extension uses the chrome.storage.local API to save your configuration settings, including extension toggle status, UI display preferences, and feature toggles. These settings are stored only on your computer to persist your preferred user experience across sessions.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. No data is collected for advertising, credit scoring, or data brokerage purposes.
